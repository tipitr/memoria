## Barkley's Executive Function Model

- **Barkley, R. A. (1997).** Behavioral inhibition, sustained attention, and executive functions: Constructing a unifying theory of ADHD. *Psychological Bulletin*, 121(1), 65–94.
  https://sciences.ucf.edu/psychology/childrenslearningclinic/wp-content/uploads/sites/24/2013/08/Barkley-1997-Psych-Bulletin.pdf
- **Barkley, R. A. (Updated model — BUEFM)** elevates working memory to a primary exogenous variable alongside behavioral inhibition.
  https://www.russellbarkley.org/factsheets/ADHD_EF_and_SR.pdf
- **Working memory + inhibition deficits in ADHD children — model comparison (2024).**
  https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC11110569/

## EndeavorRx / Akili Interactive — FDA precedent

- **FDA De Novo clearance announcement (June 2020).** First FDA-authorized digital therapeutic for any pediatric condition.
  https://www.akiliinteractive.com/news-collection/akili-announces-endeavortm-attention-treatment-is-now-available-for-children-with-attention-deficit-hyperactivity-disorder-adhd-al3pw
- **STARS trial — TOVA outcome.** 348 children, 8–12y, 25 min/day × 5 days × 4 weeks. EndeavorRx 47% improvement vs 32% educational-puzzle control.
  https://psychiatryonline.org/doi/10.1176/appi.pn.2020.8a5
- **EndeavorOTC (2024)** — first FDA clearance for adults with ADHD via game.
  https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20240618578815/en/
- **Pediatric digital therapeutics landscape review (PMC, 2025).**
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12495783/

## Transfer-effect critique (read these before claiming anything)

- **Cortese et al. — Computerized cognitive training in ADHD: meta-analysis with blinded outcomes (Mol Psychiatry, 2023).** Working memory training produces near-transfer only; no robust symptom effects with blinded raters.
  https://www.nature.com/articles/s41380-023-02000-7
- **Computerized cognitive interventions for adults with ADHD: systematic review (2023).**
  https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36892894/
- **Far-transfer effects of EF training in neurodevelopmental disorders — systematic review (2024).**
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10920464/
- **Cogmed-style WM training — meta-analytic review of cognitive, academic, behavioral outcomes.**
  https://apps.asha.org/EvidenceMaps/Articles/ArticleSummary/97271225-08aa-41ad-8ef9-4f03b772f957
- **Inhibition training transfer — RCT (2023).** Limited evidence of transfer.
  https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC10637678/
